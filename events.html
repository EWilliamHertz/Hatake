Upcoming Events

We’re excited to announce that Need to Be Geek will be participating in the following SweCard events, where we’ll be selling merchandise, including Pellekuling’s sealed products!

  • SweCard Skara: September 27, 2025, in Skara, Sweden.
  • SweCard Svedala: October 11-12, 2025, in Svedala, Sweden (just 20 minutes from Malmö).

Join us at these events to explore our range of products, including exclusive sealed products from Pellekuling, perfect for collectors and enthusiasts. SweCard is Sweden’s largest expo for trading card enthusiasts, bringing together collectors, exhibitors, and fans from across the region. Don’t miss out on this opportunity to connect with the trading card community and grab some unique items!

For more information about SweCard, visit www.swecard.org.
