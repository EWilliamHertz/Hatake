Welcome to Hatake TCG

Hatake TCG is a passionate startup based in Falköping, Sweden, dedicated to elevating the trading card game experience. We’re crafting premium accessories—matte sleeves, top-loaders, deckboxes, and duffel bags—designed for collectors and players who demand quality and style.

Our first product samples are on their way and already pre-booked by excited TCG enthusiasts! We’re gearing up for our official launch on October 15, 2025, with a curated selection of products available for pre-order now.

Our Mission

At Hatake, we aim to build a vibrant TCG community by offering high-quality, branded merchandise and fostering connections through events and partnerships. From our roots in Sweden, we’re planning to expand internationally and open a physical card shop in Alingsås by 2030.
