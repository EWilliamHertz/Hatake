Our Journey

Founded in the heart of Falköping, Sweden.

Hatake TCG is a startup born from a love for trading card games.

The vibrant community they inspire.

What began as a small venture has grown.

A brand dedicated to providing top-tier accessories.

For players and collectors alike.

But our story doesn’t stop here.

We’re on a mission to bring the TCG experience to life.

By 2030, we aim to open a physical card shop in Alingsås.

Creating a welcoming space where enthusiasts can gather.

Trade, and celebrate their passion for the game.

Join us on this exciting journey!

Whether you’re a player, collector, or someone who believes in our vision.

There’s a place for you in our story.
