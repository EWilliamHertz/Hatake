Our Journey

Founded in the heart of Falköping, Sweden, Hatake TCG is a startup born from a love for trading card games and the vibrant community they inspire.

What began as a small venture has grown into a brand dedicated to providing top-tier accessories for players and collectors alike.

Our story doesn’t stop here. We’re on a mission to bring the TCG experience to life by opening a physical card shop in Alingsås by 2030, creating a welcoming space where enthusiasts can gather, trade, and celebrate their passion.

Join us on this exciting journey! Whether you’re a player, collector, or someone who believes in our vision, there’s a place for you in our story.
