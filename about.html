About Hatake TCG

Welcome to Hatake TCG, a Swedish startup founded by passionate trading card game enthusiasts. We're launching in 2025 with a mission to provide premium, sustainable accessories for TCG players and collectors across Europe.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create products that not only protect your cards but also enhance your gaming experience. From matte sleeves to duffel bags, every item is designed with the player in mind.

Join us as we redefine TCG accessories with quality, innovation, and community at the core. Follow us on Discord to stay updated!

Design Process

Our products are designed in Sweden, with direct input from the TCG community via our Discord server (join us here). We prioritize functionality, sustainability, and style, creating accessories like our duffel bags with padded compartments tailored for tournaments.

Manufacturing

We partner with a trusted supplier in China, recommended by a Magic: The Gathering acquaintance, ensuring ethical practices and high-quality production. We oversee the process closely to maintain consistency and quality, focusing on eco-friendly materials like recyclable plastics.

Testing

Every product undergoes rigorous testing to exceed industry standards. For example, our matte sleeves are tested for shuffle durability, lasting 20% longer than leading competitors, and our deckboxes are tested for magnetic closure strength to prevent accidental openings.

Our Journey So Far
